EU Registration NA Registration

HOW TO PARTICIPATE
Sign up on the Toornament website. Connect to Diabotical District Discord. Be available to play two matches a week. Coordinate availability with league mod.

THE LEAGUE
20 players will be split into 4 groups of 5. The top 2 in each group will proceed to the knock out stage.

GAME RULES
Default Duel game mode rules with a Diabotical District League Mod present.

DIABOTICAL RANKS PERMITTED
Applicants must complete their 10 duel placement matches to obtain a duel rank. Only the lower 6 ranks are permitted to apply to the league. Explorer | Artisan | Scout | Hunter | Tactician | Sentinel

DURATION OF COMPETITION
The EU Tournament commences on 6th September, the NA on the 13th September. The Group Stages will take 14 days to complete with each player playing 2 games a week. Players must organise with their opponents in #eu-league text chat on Discord to keep a record of agreements. The Toornament website will organise the games for us and a League Mod will paste the games in Discord for you to plan. Matches booked between 6PM to 8PM GMT evening time will have a good chance of their match being cast live on community Twitch channels. Group matches can be played off stream with a League Mod if unavailable to make the Stream time slot for a match.

The Knockout Stages will be played on a Sunday 20th/27th (EU/NA) at TBD (PM). If you are not going to be available for a Sunday knockout phase please do not sign up for the league.

MAP PICKS
All Matches Except Finals are played over 3 maps. The Final is played over 5 maps. Players will pick, pick, ban, ban, pick.

Map Pool is Bioplant | Frontier | Kasbah | Perilous | Skybreak

Reporting Games
Both winner and loser should confirm the game on the Toornament website. #Match-Reports in Discord can be used to report matches to Mods with a screenshot of the final score as proof.
