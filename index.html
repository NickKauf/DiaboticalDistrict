Welcome To Diabotical District


Our community aims to give new and less eggs-perienced Diabotical players the opportunity to enjoy regular competitive match play and connect with other players to train and improve their game.

Our competitive league for players from Explorer to Sentinel rank is designed primarily for fun but it's also a great way to learn and track your progress. Matches will be cast live on Twitch. 

Together, we hope to ease the learning curve for new players and grow the community for this egg-ceptional AFPS. Happy Fragging!
